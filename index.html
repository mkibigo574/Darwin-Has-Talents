Our Vision

Stopping the Talent Drain

We are building the bridge between the high school music room and the national stage, so NT's brightest musicians never have to choose between home and their dreams.

Retention of Talent

Stop the "brain drain" of NT creatives by providing school leavers and upcoming artists with high-level opportunities to perform, record, and grow without needing to move interstate.

Territory-Wide Equity

Talent isn't just in the capital city. Our commitment reaches the Red Centre, the Big Rivers, and the Barkly regions, ensuring every young Territorian has a stage.

Industry Excellence

We aren't just a competition; we are a professional bridge. By connecting artists with mentors, technical crews, and media exposure, we raise the standard of the local music economy.

"
To empower the next generation of Northern Territory musicians by bridging the gap between education and industry. Through a territory-wide search for excellence, Darwin Has Talent provides a professional platform, mentorship, and regional pathways that celebrate local voices, retain our talent, and place the NT at the heart of the Australian music map.

— Darwin Has Talent Mission Statement

Our Story

From a Parent's Vision
to a Territory Stage

Darwin Has Talent wasn't born in a boardroom; it was born at the kitchen table and in the front row of school concerts.

As a parent of a talented Year 12 music student in Darwin, I saw firsthand the incredible raw talent our young people possess. But I also saw the "cliff" they face after graduation.

I watched my daughter and her peers wonder: "Do I have to move to the southern states to be heard?"

My answer was a definitive "No."

I believe that the Northern Territory shouldn't just be a place where talent is born — it should be a place where talent thrives.

Darwin Has Talent is that bridge.

Launching in 2027, we are taking a stage across 3,000 kilometres — from the Red Centre to the Top End — to find, mentor, and elevate the next generation of Territory stars.

Join us as we find the sound of the Territory.
